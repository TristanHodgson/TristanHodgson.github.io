Presentation on Kolmogorov Complexity

I recently gave an eight minute presentation on Kolmogorov complexity for a varied audience consisting of mathematicians ranging from first year undergraduates to professors. Due to the lack of computer science background of the audience I avoided the formalism of turing machines, to focus the talk on the more mathematically interesting aspects, such as the uncountability of the complexity function.
